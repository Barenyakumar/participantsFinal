Frequently asked questions

What do i need to get started?

1. A computer or mobile device that meets our minimum requirements.
2. A reliable internet connection (upload speed of at least 3MBps)
3. A microphone, either built-in or external
4. You must be at least 18 years old
5. Ability to speak your thoughts aloud in English

How do I get paid?

We pay contributors via UPI, NEFT. You must have a UPI account and live in a country that can receive UPI money transfers. You will receive your payment via UPI 7 days after completing your website or app test. (This waiting period allows us to ensure the quality of all recordings).

How much money I will make?

Applying to become a contributor is a great way to earn a few extra money on the side. The number of opportunities you receive will depend on companies looking for contributors with your experience. You can earn Rs.50 for every 5-minute test, Rs.100 for every 20-minute test, and up to Rs.800 for live interviews.
